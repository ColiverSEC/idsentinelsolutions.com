Overview

This lab provides hands-on experience with Microsoft Intune, the cloud-based endpoint management solution in Microsoft 365. It focuses on enrolling devices, creating configuration and compliance policies, managing applications, and integrating Conditional Access. Walkthroughs simulate real-world enterprise device management tasks, giving admins visibility and control over corporate devices.
